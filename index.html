ABOUT ME

Personal Details

Maintaining an active lifestyle fuels my mind and body, whether it's dominating the basketball court, strategizing a winning move on the cricket pitch, or spiking a powerful volleyball serve. Beyond athletics, I find myself drawn to the strategic and creative world of web development. Just like I enjoy dissecting plays on the field, I relish the challenge of breaking down complex problems into elegant lines of code. This passion for web development fuels my constant learning. Driven and hardworking with a calm demeanor, I believe these qualities, along with my adaptability and collaborative spirit, will equip me to excel in any challenge I take on. My ultimate ambition is to become a paragon of web development, a master of this ever-evolving field. I yearn to push the boundaries of what's possible, crafting innovative and user-centric experiences that leave a lasting impact.

Educational Details

My educational journey at H.K.H Public School (Primary) and Demonstration Multipurpose School (Secondary) has been a valuable experience, fostering a love for learning and active participation in extracurricular activities. While my academic performance varied across subjects, I consistently achieved passing grades. This passion for continuous learning fuels my goal to become a proficient developer.

Throughout my academic journey, I've actively sought opportunities to excel beyond academics.
